Welcoming Immigrants in Canada

A Civic Action Reflection Project

Project Goal

The goal of this project was to help immigrants feel accepted in the community, raise awareness about the challenges they face, and encourage people to be kind and welcoming toward newcomers.

The Issue

Many immigrants do not always feel accepted or supported when they arrive in Canada.

Actions Taken

I created a website to raise awareness and collect opinions from others.

Results & Impact

The project helped raise awareness and encouraged people to be more welcoming.

Challenges & Learning

I learned how important immigration is to Canada’s economy and how important support systems are.

Reflection & Future Action

In the future, I plan to stay informed and help new students feel welcomed.
