Welcoming Immigrants in Canada

This website was created as part of a civic action project to raise awareness about immigration in Canada, explain the actions taken, and reflect on the results.

The Issue

What problem in the community does this project focus on?

Many immigrants in Canada do not always feel accepted or fully supported when they arrive. Even though immigrants play a major role in Canada’s growth and economy, they often face challenges such as limited access to resources, difficulty getting their qualifications recognized, and feeling excluded from the community.

This issue matters because without proper support and acceptance, immigrants may struggle to succeed, which affects not only them but also the overall strength of Canadian society.

The Action

What action was taken to address the issue?

The main action taken for this project was creating this website. The purpose of the website is to raise awareness about immigration, encourage people to reflect on their attitudes toward newcomers, and provide a space for others to share their opinions.

Using a website as an action allowed the message to reach more people and made it easier for others to engage with the topic by reading, reflecting, and leaving comments.

The Results

What impact did the action have?

The website helped raise awareness about the challenges immigrants face and encouraged people to think more carefully about how they treat newcomers in their community. The comments and feedback received showed that visitors were reflecting on the issue and reconsidering their views.

Overall, this project had a positive impact by starting conversations and promoting kindness, understanding, and support for immigrants.
